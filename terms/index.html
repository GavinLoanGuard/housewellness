Service Description

House Wellness provides visual home wellness checkups for residential properties in Calgary and surrounding areas. Our service includes a visual inspection of major home systems and a written report of our findings. We do not perform repairs, code compliance inspections, or real estate transaction inspections.

What We Are Not

House Wellness is not a licensed home inspection service for real estate transactions. Our checkups are maintenance-focused assessments for existing homeowners. We do not provide engineering assessments, code compliance verification, or specialized testing (such as radon, mold, or asbestos testing) unless explicitly stated.

Scheduling and Payment

Payment is due upon completion of the checkup. We accept credit card, debit, e-transfer, or cash. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled appointment may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Limitations of Visual Inspection

Our checkup is a visual inspection only. We do not move furniture, personal belongings, or stored items. We do not perform destructive testing or open walls, ceilings, or floors. Some conditions may not be visible or detectable through a visual inspection.

Report and Recommendations

Our report represents our professional opinion based on conditions observed at the time of the checkup. Recommendations are for informational purposes only. You should consult with qualified professionals before undertaking any repairs or improvements.

Limitation of Liability

House Wellness liability is limited to the fee paid for the checkup. We are not liable for any consequential, incidental, or special damages arising from our services or report.

Access Requirements

The homeowner or an authorized adult (18+) must be present during the checkup. The homeowner is responsible for providing reasonable access to all areas of the property, including the attic, crawl spaces, electrical panel, and mechanical systems.
