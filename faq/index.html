Frequently Asked Questions

About the Service

Is this the same as a home inspection?
No. Home inspections are for real estate transactions ($500-700). House Wellness is a proactive maintenance checkup for homeowners who want to stay ahead of problems.
Do you do repairs?
No, we only inspect. We never do repairs ourselves. If you want help finding a contractor for something we identify, we can connect you with trusted pros we've vetted. There's no pressure to use them.
Can you recommend contractors?
Yes. We've built relationships with reliable contractors across different trades. If your report identifies something that needs attention, just ask and we'll make an introduction. You're never obligated to use them.
How long does it take?
About 2 hours for most homes. We do not rush.
Do I need to be home?
Yes, someone 18+ should be present so we can explain findings in real-time.

Winter & Scheduling

How do you inspect the roof with snow?
We do not climb on snowy roofs. For winter bookings, we inspect interior systems immediately and return for a free spring visit to inspect the roof once snow melts.
How far in advance do I need to book?
Most checkups are scheduled within 5-7 days. During peak seasons it may take a bit longer.

Pricing & Payment

How much does it cost?
$249 flat rate. No tiers, no add-ons, no surprises.
When do I pay?
After the checkup, not before. We accept credit card, debit, e-transfer, or cash.
What areas do you serve?
Calgary and surrounding communities: Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, Okotoks. Same price, no travel fees.
