We Know Calgary Homes

From 1950s Beltline bungalows to brand-new builds in Seton, we understand the unique challenges Calgary homes face.

Clay Soil Foundations

Calgary's expansive clay swells and shrinks seasonally, stressing foundations. We check for cracks, moisture, and settlement.

Chinook Stress

Rapid temperature changes cause expansion/contraction damage. We inspect seals, caulking, and weather stripping.

Ice Dam Prevention

Our attic inspection checks insulation, ventilation, and ice dam indicators before they cause damage.
