Frequently asked questions

Is this the same as a home inspection?
No. Home inspections are for real estate transactions. House Wellness is a preventative maintenance checkup for homeowners who want to understand their home's condition.
Do you do repairs?
No, we only assess and document. If you need repairs, we can connect you with contractors we've vetted and trust. There's no obligation to use them.
What if you find something serious?
We tell you immediately during the assessment and recommend appropriate next steps. If you want help finding a reliable contractor, we're happy to make an introduction.
How long does it take?
Approximately 2 hours for a typical home. We do not rush.
