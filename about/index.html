Our Philosophy

Why "No Repairs" Matters

Most home service companies have a conflict of interest. They inspect your home, then try to sell you repairs. The more problems they "find," the more money they make.

We do it differently. Our technicians don't do repairs. They aren't paid commission. They have no financial incentive to exaggerate or upsell.

That means you get honest answers. If something needs attention, we tell you. If it can wait, we tell you that too. And if you need a contractor, we'll connect you with our vetted network‚Äîbut we don't take kickbacks.
