About Us

Why We Started House Wellness

Helping Calgary homeowners stay ahead of expensive repairs.

After 20+ years running businesses in Calgary, I kept seeing the same pattern: homeowners discovering problems only after they became expensive emergencies.

A furnace that could have been replaced in September for $4,000 becomes a $7,000 emergency in January. A slow leak that started as a $300 repair becomes $15,000 in water damage.

House Wellness exists to break that cycle. We help you catch problems early, understand what needs attention, and plan repairs on your timeline instead of reacting to emergencies.

We don't do repairs ourselves. But when you need work done, we can connect you with contractors we've vetted and trust. No pressure, just helpful introductions if you want them.

Gavin
Founder, House Wellness
